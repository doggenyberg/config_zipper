With this program, you are able to copy your CS:GO config files faster. Make sure to know where your config files are stored before using this program.

  1. 1

    Import your CS:GO config folder. The program will zip the necessary files and save them to your computer.

  2. 2

    Assuming you will play on another computer, you will have to save the zipped folder manually. For example to a USB stick or cloud storage.

  3. 3

    Upload the zipped folder to your game folder on the new/other computer.

Where is the CS:GO folder located?

The folder is stored as mentioned below, per default.

C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\userdata\STEAMID\730\local\cfg

How do I find my SteamID?

The easiest way to find out is to:

  1. Open Steam and navigate to your profile's inventory.
  2. Click the blue "Trade Offers" button in the upper right-hand corner.
  3. Scroll down to "Third-Party Sites" by "Who can send me Trade Offers".
  4. You will see a URL here. The number between "?partner=" and "#&token" is your SteamID.
